One of the most popular ways of making money is through buying and selling stocks on the Stock Market. This method is used by many, from a simple worker trying to make some extra side money to large teams in Banks and in Hedge Funds.

In order to make a profit, investors need to have a strategy, which will give them clear indicators as to when to buy a stock, when to collect profits and when to take losses in a losing trade. There are many of these strategies which are been used frequently, however, they all require the investor to find stocks which fulfil the criteria of a given strategy by themselves. This is an extremely tedious process, given that it requires a trader to look through as many stocks as possible, which numbered 58,200 at the end of Q1 in 2022 , and because the prices of each of these stocks (which to an extent determines whether to buy the stock) changes every few seconds whilst the markets are open.

It also means that anyone who has entered into a trade must constantly check the price of the stocks which they have bought, in order to determine whether their strategy suggests that it is time to sell. The frequency with which a stock must be checked upon is usually once a day for stocks bought with an investing strategy, and up to multiple times an hour with trading strategies , especially those in the category of day trading .

This makes the job of investors even more complex, as they must compare every stock to every strategy (which they are comfortable with using), in order to maximise profit. This inevitably reduces the amount of time available to search through more stocks, once again capping investor’s ability to make more money.

Furthermore, making money through the stock market requires a person to have a proper understanding of how the stock market works, commonly used terminology and knowledge of how to use investing & trading strategies profitably. This greatly limits the amount of people using the stock market, meaning that the prices of stocks are lower than they could be (due to the demand for stocks not being at its maximum potential), and hence a shared prosperity is somewhat prevented.

It is for these reasons that I am creating this project, a stock market analyser. This programme will import stock data for thousands of stocks from popular APIs, store them in a database, and then apply multiple trading/investing strategies to each one of them, displaying a list of stocks which it recommends to buy, and similarly a list of stocks to sell (in order to prevent of losses) – all displayed on a website, which is the easiest way for users to view it. This solves the problems related above, as it removes the need for tedious searching through thousands of stocks, it severely reduces the time required to check whether action is needed for already owned stocks, and it ends the need for someone to understand the stock market and its strategies in order to make money.
